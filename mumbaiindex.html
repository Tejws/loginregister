Introduction

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It's not just a city, it's an experience. Known as the "City of Dreams", Mumbai is a bustling metropolis, a melting pot of cultures, and India's financial, commercial, and entertainment hub.

Transportation

Mumbai has an extensive transportation network, including suburban trains, metro, buses, and a robust taxi system. The Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport connects the city to the world.

Tourism

Mumbai attracts tourists with its historic landmarks, cultural festivals, and the allure of Bollywood. The city offers a mix of modern luxury hotels and budget accommodations.

Economy and Business

Mumbai's economy is diverse, with a strong presence in finance, entertainment, information technology, and more. It's a global business hub with numerous multinational corporations headquartered here.
